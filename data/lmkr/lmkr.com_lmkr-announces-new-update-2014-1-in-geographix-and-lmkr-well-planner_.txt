LMKR announces new update 2014.1 in GeoGraphix and LMKR Well Planner
LMKR
May 27, 2014
LMKR today announced the newest update to its popular E&P Interpretation software GeoGraphix® 2014 and new complimentary LMKR product, LMKR Well Planner.  This latest release provides a host of new features and enhancements to GeoGraphix 2014 and fixes some known issues.  LMKR Well Planner has been updated with exciting new features to make the well planning experience even easier.
Today’s 2014.1 update includes the following new features and fixes reported known issues:
Advanced 3D Visualization
Advanced Field Planning
Well Planner
Reported Issues
“The newest release of GeoGraphix and LMKR Well Planner underlines our commitment to ensuring that we are providing solutions relevant to our market and customers.  The GeoGraphix team is here to listen and is committed to continuing to deliver solutions which offer  new and faster methods of finding and exploiting hydrocarbons, while reducing cycle time and driving down production costs. Our 2014.1 update provides the G&G professional with  new features and enhancements in   advanced 3D visualization & field planning capabilities that result in real reductions in time and cost.”
Stan Abele, Vice President Product Management LMKR
Availability LMKR’s 2014.1 release update is available worldwide from May 27, 2014. Visit our website for more information and to download.
LMKR is a petroleum technology company with an extensive solutions portfolio that includes reservoir-centric interpretation, modeling and analytics software, mobile technology solutions, E&P data services as well as geoscience and information management consulting solutions.
