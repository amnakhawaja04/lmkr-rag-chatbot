LMK 2023 Townhall: A Year of Expanding Horizons
LMKR
At the LMK 2023 Wrap-up Gala, CEO & Chairman LMKR Atif Rais Khan and President Iftikhar Atif Khan highlighted the company’s achievements of the current year. They also outlined a visionary outlook for the future.
The update showcased strategic partnerships with industry leaders such as MasterCard, Bank of Khyber, IKON Geosciences, RESPEC, and SRTI Park. This underscored LMK’s commitment to impactful collaborations.
Within LMK’s incubation ecosystem , over 500 startups were incubated. A successful partnership with INSEAD for the XSeed Incubation Program at NIC Karachi emphasized the company’s significant role in nurturing entrepreneurship.
Celebrating a major milestone, LMK acknowledged the seamless transportation of 80 million passengers through the BRT system. This achievement solidifies LMK’s dedication to pioneering excellence in transportation solutions.
Atif concluded the meetup by sharing,
In 2023, we witnessed incredible dedication and innovation at the heart of LMK. Together, we faced challenges head-on, reached new heights, and pushed boundaries. The journey is ongoing, and I’m confident that our path will lead us to even greater success in the years ahead.
Iftikhar added,
LMK’s success is a result of our collective efforts. Our partnerships, incubation initiatives, and groundbreaking projects showcase the true strength of our collaborative spirit. The road ahead is exciting, filled with possibilities and opportunities that we eagerly anticipate.
Beyond the townhall
Following the impactful townhall, attendees immersed themselves in a day filled with outdoor activities and entertainment. Games like Human-sized Foosball, Snakes and Ladders tic-tac-toe, as well as badminton and tug of war fostered a spirit of camaraderie within the team.
The festivities continued with a comedy one-man show, ‘No Offence’ by Dawar Hussain, injecting humor and joy into the day. The event concluded on a high note with an exquisite lunch accompanied by live music, creating a perfect blend of professional success and joyous celebration.
These moments highlighted not just LMK’s achievements but also its commitment to fostering a positive and inclusive workplace. The Wrap-up Gala showcased the essence that makes LMK a unique and thriving group.
