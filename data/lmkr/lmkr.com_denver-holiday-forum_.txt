Denver Holiday Forum
LMKR
December 5, 2014
Over 100 Denver-based clients representing 38 companies joined LMKR for a GeoGraphix Holiday Cocktail Reception at Fogo De Chao in Denver. Vince Molliconi, Vice President Global Sales and Julie Daughtery, Sales Manager along with LMKR Product Managers hosted the event. Clients enjoyed drinks and hors d’oeuvres as they discussed the upcoming GeoGraphix 2014 release with LMKR team members. Participants were excited to learn more about the advanced features of GeoGraphix 2014.
