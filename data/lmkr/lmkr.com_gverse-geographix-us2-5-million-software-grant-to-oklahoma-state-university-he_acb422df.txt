GVERSE GeoGraphix US$2.5 million software grant to Oklahoma State University helps students get practical experience of working with advanced geological and geophysical software
LMKR
April 3, 2020
GVERSE GeoGraphix delivers a grant of US $2.5 million to students at the Oklahoma State University. As part of LMKR’s University Grant Program, GVERSE GeoGraphix licenses are provided to students to give them a hands-on experience of geological software used in the industry.
Exposing students to real life situations and job opportunities, the main focus of LMKR’s University Grant Program is giving students access to some of the most powerful geological and geophysical software packages used in the industry and ensure students have had every possible advantage when entering the workforce.
