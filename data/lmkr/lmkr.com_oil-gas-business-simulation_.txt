A Professional Training Simulation
XpoSim is a next generation training simulation for the petroleum industry. It is a unique product that brings together key phases and activities of the exploration lifecycle. It offers invaluable insights for both professionals and students, exposing them to real-world scenarios supported by actual geological, geophysical and petrophysical data.
Request a demo to learn about XpoSim and how a practical simulation solution can help you enhance your knowledge in real business settings.
