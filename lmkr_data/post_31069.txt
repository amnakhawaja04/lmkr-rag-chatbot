Title: LMKR, Oracle Strengthen Cloud Relationship
Date: 2025-10-28T06:45:52

Dubai, October 2025 — LMKR proudly announced a major milestone in its cloud transformation journey with the signing of a new collaboration with Oracle at GITEX Global 2025 . The announcement marks LMKR’s adoption of Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) to power its next generation of digital platforms across multiple sectors including Oil & Gas, Transportation, Construction Management, and Cloud Consulting . This collaboration reinforces LMKR’s continued commitment to delivering innovative, secure, and high-performance enterprise solutions for clients in Pakistan and around the globe. Collaboration Highlights Through this relationship, LMKR will leverage Oracle’s globally trusted cloud capabilities to: Modernize and optimize enterprise systems hosted on LMKR’s platforms Strengthen reliability, security, and cost-efficiency through OCI Boost scalability and performance for mission-critical applications Support the expansion of LMKR offerings into the GCC and international markets The ceremony took place at the Oracle booth during GITEX, attended by senior leadership from both organizations. A Shared Cloud Vision This collaboration reflects LMKR’s close engagement with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) to drive scalability, performance, and innovation across its global product portfolio. Working in close coordination, both teams have aligned architectures, validated performance benchmarks, and established a resilient foundation for multi-region deployments that will support LMKR’s next generation of digital platforms. This milestone accelerates LMKR’s digital transformation journey and reinforces a shared commitment to innovation, operational excellence, and continuous growth.
