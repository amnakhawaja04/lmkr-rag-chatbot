Title: LMKR wins big at P@SHA ICT Awards
Date: 2022-11-03T07:32:04

November 3, 2022 Trverse shines at the 18 th P@SHA ICT Awards, continues its winning streak. LMKR won awards in three categories including Consumers, Industrial and Public Sector. Each year, P@SHA awards seek out tech leaders and the unsung heroes of Pakistan’s IT industry to acknowledge their contribution and impact to the IT industry, Pakistan’s economy, and wider world. LMKR Group’s success at P@SHA ICT Awards is not new; LMKT bagged five and six awards in 2018, and 2019 respectively and also won an award for its Zu App in 2021. This year, LMKR won the awards for some of its most recognized solutions including Automated Fare Collection (AFC), Open Marketplace and IITS. The smart ticketing solution AFC earlier won an award at Transport Ticketing Global in London as well. Speaking about the significant win, Vice President Technology Business & Alliances, LMKR, Nasir Tanveer said, It is always a pleasure to be a part of P@SHA Awards as getting recognition from our very own fraternity is very special indeed. Under the Trverse brand LMKR is fundamentally changing the urban mobility landscape of Pakistan. While we are immensely proud of what we have achieved so far, I can assure you that this is just the beginning. Here is the breakdown of the awards: Open Marketplace – Marketplaces, Consumer IITS – Transport, Industrial AFC – Government & Citizen Service, Public Sector
