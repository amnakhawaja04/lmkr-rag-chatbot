Title: LMKR GeoGraphix® Contract enters into a 2 – year renewal period
Date: 2015-07-27T14:45:25

July 27, 2015 LMKR today announced that the exclusive distributor rights to GeoGraphix® granted by Landmark Graphics Corporation to LMKR will, pursuant to the terms of the License Agreement between Landmark and LMKR, enter into its 2 year renewal period commencing on August 16, 2015.  Termination of the renewal term will consequently be August 15, 2017.  Please note, however, that notwithstanding the termination of the License Agreement upon the expiration of the renewal term, LMKR retains all rights to continue to license and service GeoGraphix® for each LMKR licensees having an effective license of Geographix® as of the date of such termination until the expiration of such respective license.
